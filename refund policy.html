Refund Policy

This refund policy applies to fees and payments made for courses and programs offered by our college.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may be granted under the following circumstances:

  • If a course or program is canceled by the college, a full refund will be issued.
  • If a student withdraws from a course or program before a specified date, a refund may be available according to the college's refund schedule.
  • If a student is unable to attend a course or program due to extenuating circumstances, a refund may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact the college's administration office or registrar to initiate the refund process.
  2. Provide the necessary information, including proof of payment and any supporting documentation.
  3. Refunds will be processed within a specified timeframe, and the method of refund will be determined by the college.

Non-Refundable Fees

Some fees are non-refundable and will not be eligible for a refund. These may include application fees, administrative fees, and certain program-specific fees. Please refer to the specific course or program details for more information.

By enrolling in our courses or programs, you agree to comply with this refund policy.
